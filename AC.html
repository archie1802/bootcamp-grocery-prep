Avocado toast!

The classic California breakfast

1

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 pinch of salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Put a slice of bread in the toaster
  2. Once bread is evenly toasted on each side spread the avocado on the toast
  3. Apply salt and pepper as needed and enjoy!

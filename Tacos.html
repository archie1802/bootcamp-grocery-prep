Tacos!

A great tasty dinner!

Serving size: 1

Ingredients

  • 5 tortillas
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 3 cups of shredded lettuce
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 handful of shredded cheese
  • 3 scoops of sour cream

Instructions

  1. Cook ground beef
  2. Chop of the tomato
  3. Distribute ingredients amongst tacos

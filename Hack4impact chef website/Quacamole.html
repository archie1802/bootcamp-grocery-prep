Guacamole!

Another essential dip!

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 tomatoe
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 lime
  • 1 tablespoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Mash avocados in a bowl
  2. Stir in chopped tomato and onion
  3. Mince garlic and add to bowl
  4. Juice lime and add to bowl
  5. Add salt
  6. Chill in fridge for half an hour and enjoy!

Salsa

A must have dip for any party

Ingredients

  • 4 tomatoes
  • 2 jalepenos
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon of salf

Instructions

  1. Chop up the tomatoes, jalepeno, onion, and cilantro and put everything in a blender
  2. Juice the lemon into the blender
  3. Add salt into the blender
  4. Blend until its the consistency you want
  5. Enjoy!
